💬 Who We Are

We're a Toronto-based boutique blinds & shades company run by two humans who still believe style and practicality can live happily ever after. We're the team that measures, carries, installs, solves, smiles, and occasionally reschedules — all in the name of better windows and happier spaces.

👥 Meet the Team

Hakan Sutluoglu

Chief Everything Officer

In charge of installation, snacks, and overly optimistic promises.

Hakan is the guy with the tape measure, drill, and a pocket full of granola bars. From measurements to mounting, he handles it all — usually with a smile and sometimes with timelines that are… let's say "ambitious." If something's getting done today, he's probably doing it right now.

Matt Kuzhan

Chief Everything-Else Officer

In charge of excuses — from bad weather and broken logistics to the intern.

Matt is the calm behind the chaos. When orders get delayed, suppliers go silent, or the weather has other plans — he's the one crafting polite, blame-free explanations. He also handles the digital side of operations, customer communication, and all the stuff you didn't know you needed until it went wrong.

Masal Sutluoglu, age 6.

Junior Chaos Consultant

In charge of glitter spills, color opinions, and spontaneous snack inspections.

Masal is the youngest (and arguably the most expressive) member of the team. She brings bold creative direction, such as insisting all blinds should come in pink — or at least sparkle. When not performing surprise quality checks on cookies, she provides strong opinions on what "looks fun" and what "needs more unicorns." She sometimes accompanies us on visits and likes to supervise us :)

Things People Wonder About

How long does this whole thing take?

Custom blinds take 2-3 weeks to make (they're worth the wait, promise). Installation? Usually done in under 3 hours — unless Masal is supervising, then add 30 minutes for unicorn discussions.

Do you really do everything yourselves?

Absolutely. No random contractors showing up at your door. It's always us — the same faces you met during consultation. We're like a very specific, window-focused superhero team.

What if something breaks?

We've got you covered with a 3-year warranty on everything. If something goes wonky, we'll fix it. No drama, no hassle, just solutions.

Can I really get pink sparkly blinds?

While Masal would approve, we typically recommend more... timeless options. But hey, it's your space! We'll help you find something that makes both you and your inner 6-year-old happy.
